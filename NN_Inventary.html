Privacy Policy

How NN Fashions Inventary Management handles business and customer data.

  • NN Fashions Inventary Management stores business data such as products, invoices, suppliers, customers, payments, and expenses for app functionality.
  • Customer phone numbers, addresses, and invoice details are used only to support business operations and record management workflows.
  • The application does not sell customer data or share business records with third parties except where a business user explicitly configures connected services.
  • The app does not provide contact scraping or unsolicited outreach tooling.
  • Data may be stored locally on device databases and may also be connected to cloud database infrastructure when the business enables cloud mode.
  • Only the information required for inventory, billing, reporting, and business management is processed.
  • Users remain responsible for the customer data they enter and for ensuring lawful use of business records.
  • If a customer requests data correction or removal, the business using the application should update or delete that data from its records.
